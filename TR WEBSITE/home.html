ABOUT


We created this club as 3 friends who enjoyed playing together,
we then played just for fun in the Telenorliga.br
The season did not last that long since we had to withdraw,
however in January 2018 we put up a player application
on Gamer.no, which 10 people replied to.
This was the beginning of our Rocket League team.

After the foundation of this team we entered
into an agreement with Røros Infonett AS,
and exactly this deal is what made the
team start to blossom.

After another month we took over Frantic E-Sports,
which then became Frantic TR E-Sports and also
our first Norwegian Overwatch team.

After the success of an Overwatch team we attempted
to start up a Counterstrike: Global Offensive team,
but sadly without any luck.

We then entered into an sponsor agreement with Tolga Os Sparebank,
which is our main sponsor, which led to an international Overwatch team,
who has made huge progress.
